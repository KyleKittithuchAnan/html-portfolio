About Me

My background portrays a person who may not be naturally gifted but is dedicated and perseveres. I grew up in Sikhoraphum District, Surin Province, Thailand. Where the local school wasn't among the province's top institutions. Despite this, I excelled academically and was placed in the top three classes throughout high school. This achievement granted me the opportunity to attend Mahidol University, one of the country's prestigious institutions, where I earned a bachelor's degree in Physics Science (B.Sc.).

Following graduation, I embarked on a teaching career, tutoring middle and high school students in math and science for 3-4 months before transitioning to the IT field. My first role was as a software developer, despite lacking prior coding knowledge. Through connections and self-directed learning, I quickly acquired the necessary skills. Within a year, my efforts were rewarded with a promotion and an 80% salary increase, aligning with the standard IT market rates in Thailand.

After another year, I sought new challenges and joined a larger company, collaborating with international colleagues. This role demanded expertise in JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Node.js, Node-RED, Elastic Stack (Elasticsearch, Logstash, Kibana), Docker, Amazon Web Services (S3, Lambda, API Gateway, Kubernetes), and Git. I also gained experience in building chatbots for diverse industries, accumulating over four years of IT experience.

Seeking further opportunities, my family and I relocated to Calgary, Alberta, Canada, just over a year ago. While I continue to pursue IT opportunities, I've embraced every chance, whether volunteer or entry-level, while also working as a cook at a Foothill Medical Centre to support my family. Even after work, I dedicate myself to continuous learning, studying to enhance my skills and broaden my knowledge. Currently, I'm focused on acquiring full-stack development skills and plan to further my education in Python, Go, DevOps, and DevSecOps.
